# Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

## What data we collect

Before Your Advisor collects the minimum data needed to operate the service, including account email, uploaded financial statement data, and generated analysis outputs.

## How we use data

- Data is used only to provide core product functionality.
- Data is not sold or rented.
- Data is not used for advertising.
- Uploaded financial data is not used to train generalized AI models.

## Sharing

Data may be processed by infrastructure providers strictly as needed to operate the service.

## Retention and control

Data is retained while your account remains active or as required for security and compliance. You can stop using the service at any time and request deletion support through official support channels.
